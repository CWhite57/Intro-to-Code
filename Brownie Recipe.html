Ingredients

  • 185g Unsalted Butter
  • 185g Dark Chocolate
  • 85g Plain Flour
  • 40g Cocoa Powder
  • 50g White Chocolate
  • 50g Milk Chocolate
  • 3 Large Eggs
  • 275g Golden Caster Sugar

Method

  1. Cut the 185g Unsalted Butter & 185g Dark chocolate into small cubes and place into a bowl
  2. Quarter fill a saucepan with water. Sit the bowl of butter and chocolate onto the saucepan. Heat on a low heat to melt and mix, stirring ocassionally
  3. Remove bowl from pan. Leave to cool to room temp.
  4. At this point, pre heat oven to 180C/160C fan/ gas4
  5. Using a shaloow tin, cut non-stick baking paper to fit.
  6. Sieve the flour and cocoa powder into a bowl, getting rid of any lumps
  7. etc.....
